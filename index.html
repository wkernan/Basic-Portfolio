About Me

I've lived in Austin since 2001. Studied Actuarial Science at the University of Texas from 2004 to 2008. After graduating I became an 8th grade math / algebra teacher at Clint Small Middle School. Some hobbies of mine include: running, disc golf, ultimate, pc modding, gaming and eating.

I've always been curious about what makes computers work and more so about how the internet functions. This curiosity led me to teach myself web development. In particular about MVC, test driven deployement and the full stack of building dynamic websites. I started with Ruby on Rails and since then I've been teaching myself whatever language or library I can get my hands on. A few to note: Javascript, Ruby, Rails, PHP, AngularJS, API's, Git, HTML, CSS, Jade and Stylus.
